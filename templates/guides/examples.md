## 예시 1: 제품 홍보 Shorts

```
9:16 vertical video, portrait mode for mobile screen.
Cinematic, hyper-realistic style with soft studio lighting.
A sleek wireless earbuds case slowly rotating on a minimalist white
pedestal. The earbuds case is matte black with subtle metallic accents.
Slow dolly-in camera movement from medium shot to close-up.
Clean white studio background with soft shadows. Keep the product
centered, leaving bottom 20% clear for text overlay.
Soft ambient electronic music, subtle whoosh sound effects.
Duration: 8 seconds.
```

## 예시 2: 여행/풍경 Shorts

```
9:16 vertical video format, ideal for Instagram Reels.
Cinematic, vibrant colors with golden hour lighting.
A solo traveler walking along the edge of Santorini cliffs,
wearing a flowing white dress. Camera follows from behind with
 a slow tilt up revealing the sunset over the caldera.
Mediterranean architecture with white buildings and blue domes
in the background. Dreamy, wanderlust mood.
Soft acoustic guitar music, gentle ocean waves ambient sound.
Keep subject centered, safe zone at bottom for captions.
```
